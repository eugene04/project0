Climate change

The issue of climate change is the greatest threat to the survival of our species that we face today.

In Zimbabwe climate change has led to extremely high temperatures in summer which has been hard on infants and the elderly.

There have been major economic costs due to draught and falling water levels in lakes due to global warming. Read here

Solutions to this problem can only come from the richer nations who are responsible for most of the green house gas emissions. The recent withdrawal from the Paris agreement is a giant step backwards in our fight against global warming
