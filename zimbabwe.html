Political climate

Zimbabwe’s first and only party to rule Zimbabwe ZANU PF came to power after a civil war between the white ruling elite and the majority blacks fighting for equal rights among the races. The fact that Zimbabwe has been ruled by a liberation party has led to a situation where our liberators have become our oppressors since they believe they are entitled to elitist privileges for their role in our liberation.

Our political space has been characterised by repression of the political players who do not have liberation war credentials and this has led to violence and questionable election outcomes that have not been recognised as free and fair by the international community.
